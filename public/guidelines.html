Community Guideline

1

Learnion community is for people learning languages and cultures via user-generated content. The intention is to generate healthy and educational discussion so that everyone learns something from the other. In order to do so, it is important to take into consideration the following:

Guidelines

2

      Treat others as you want to be treated.
      Everyone has different opinions, be tolerant and respectful.
      When commenting, do so with courtesy and respect.
      No spam permitted.
      Do not post sexual content or violent content.
      Do not post personal information.
      Keep self promotion to a minimum.
      Breaking these rules will result in a ban and depending on the severity it may be permanent or not.

Agreement

By using the ‘community section’ you agree to these guidelines.
