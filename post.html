What is the community section?


In this section you can see posts and create your own posts to share. You can also comment and reply to comments. When you upload something you must wait for approval.
For a post you need the following:

Post fields


  • Post title
  • Body text
  • Image (optional)
  • Post type (standard or image)
  • Content package (share your words, phrases and/or grammar structures)

Enjoy and keep learning

**Stick to the community guidelines when posting
